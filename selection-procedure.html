Yatri Selection Procedure

A rigorous process is in place to select the brightest talent from a large pool of applicants. The idea is to identify youth over 21 years of age, who have the potential to lead change.

Applicants can apply online

Apply Now

Criteria – Abilities, not Marksheets

To select a candidate, we do not look at exam-results; rather we consider abilities, motivations and values of the candidate. We consider the candidates’ understanding of India’s pressing problems and the commitment and approach to solving these.

Diversity in Selection

While aiming for the best candidates from all over India, our focus remains on Middle India, and an even geographic spread within India. We also aim for equal representation of male-female candidates. Jagriti Yatra includes upto 40 exceptional international candidates whose global exposure adds to the richness of the Yatra.

The Yatri Composition

Yatris selected for the Yatra can either be a ‘Participant’ or a ‘Facilitator’.

Facilitators are experienced professionals, over 28 years of age, who are keen to join the movement for Enterprise-led development. They may be entrepreneurs, aspiring entrepreneurs or other professionals.

A facilitator plays the dual role of a mentor and coordinator during the Yatra and is assigned a cohort of 6 participants to:

  • Support the participants to make the most of the Yatra by managing individual and collaborative learning
  • Manage the cohort throughout the journey as per the Yatra Mantra (Code of Conduct)
  • Act as a bridge between the participants and the organizing team

Responsibilities of the facilitators require them to be fluent in either English or Hindi languages.

Participants are young candidates above 21 years who want to ‘be the change they want to see’ and have the potential to become entrepreneurs and change-makers.

Selection

Interested candidates may apply online by filling up the main application form

Interested candidates may apply online by filling up the main application form The application consists of a series of essay-type questions designed to ascertain your suitability for the Yatra. It is a subjective evaluation of the raw entrepreneurial potential within a candidate by a selection panel of more than 30 experts spread across the globe. Random interviews and reference checks are also conducted to ascertain veracity of candidature. 

Facilitators' applications are further evaluated on their professional experience and ability to communicate, coordinate and manage youth. This is usually done through additional information in the form and an informal telephonic interview. 

Once your application is selected, further financial details are required if you’re seeking a scholarship, to help us identify deserving candidates.

Round 1 : Submit your application
Steps to Join the Yatra

1. Apply Now: Click on the "Apply Now" button to access the registration form.

2. Submit Registration Form: Fill out the registration form and submit it. You'll receive an email to verify your email account.

3. Activate Account: To activate your account, simply click on the verification link provided in the email.

4. Login: Visit the Jagriti Yatra website and log in using your registered email address and the password you set during registration.

5. Pay Processing Fee: Complete the payment of INR 100 as the Jagriti Yatra application processing fee.

6. Access Application: After successful payment, your application form will become accessible. Click on "Edit Application Form" to open it. The application consists of three sections:

- Personal Details
- Enterprise Involvement
- General Aptitude

7. Complete Application: Fill out the application form following the provided instructions in each section.

8. Submit Application: Once you've filled out all sections, submit your application.

ROUND 2

Upon being shortlisted, you qualify for the next round of the selection process.

ROUND 2: Video Interview

1. Receive Notification: Keep an eye on your email inbox. If you're shortlisted in the first round, you'll receive an email notifying you of your qualification for the next stage.

2. Login to JY website : Access the Jagriti Yatra Portal and locate the video interview link. Click on the link and Log in using your registered email ID and phone number, then verify your identity with an OTP.

Note: Ensure that your login credentials match those registered on the Jagriti Yatra Portal.

3. Conduct Video Interview: Complete your video interview according to the provided instructions.

4. Submit Interview: After finishing the interview, submit it through the portal.

5. Confirmation: Await confirmation of your interview submission. You'll receive an acknowledgment via email or WhatsApp. Capture a screenshot of this confirmation for your records.

6. Submission: Log in to the Jagriti Yatra portal and upload the screenshot taken. Your application is fully submitted.

*Note: You can submit a screenshot of either an email or WhatsApp message confirming your submission.*

The Application Process

Applicants can apply online Application Link

Applications are accepted in English and Hindi. There is Rs 100/- application fee for applying. The relevant Yatra fees are applicable only if you’re selected.

Online Application

Start your application by registering on https://jagritiyatra.com/registration.php . 

You can select your choice of language at the time of registration. Candidates applying in Hindi don’t need any special software or hardware to fill in the form. We have tried to make this as simple as typing in English. 

Filling up the application form requires you to think carefully about your answers. For this, if you don’t have easy access to the internet, you may note down the questions and work on your application offline. You can come back to type out or copy-paste your final answers in one go.

Scholarship

Candidates who apply for scholarships are carefully assessed and scholarships are granted to the most deserving. 

We aim to support all the Yatris through various levels of scholarship.

Scholarship opportunities are awarded on the basis of merit and financial need of the candidate. Selected applicants will need to fill up the additional Scholarship Form if they wish to apply for a scholarship in their application. All the candidates seeking a scholarship must submit supporting official income documents. 

We also urge selected Yatris to look for alternative sources of financial aid. We have a brief guide to help Yatris in approaching funding sources like trusts, colleges and corporations to raise their own fees.
